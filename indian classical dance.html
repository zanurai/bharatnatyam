BHARATNATYAM:


Bharatnatyam is a major form of indian classcial dance that originated in Tamil Nadu. The natya shastra by bharata Muni and Abhinaya Darpana by Nandikeshvara are considered to be the original sources of bharatnatyam. THe dance form is also written by Smeshwara. It has flourished in the temples and couts of southern India since ancient times. It is one of the eight widely recognized Indian classical dance forms, and expresses South Indian religious themes and spiritual ideas particularly oa shaivism, Vaishnavism, and Shaktism, collectively Hinduism.A discription od bharatnatyam from the 2nd century CE can be found in the ancient Tamil epic SIlappatikaram, while temple sculptures of the 6th to 9th century CE suggest it was a highly refine performance art by the mid-1st millennium CE. Bharatnatyam is the oldest classcial dance tradition in India. BHaratnatyam reminded exclusive to hindu temples through the 19th century.
